## 🎨 The 3-Layer Audio System

### Professional Audio Structure

**Layer 1: Dialogue/Narration** (Foundation)
- Volume: 100% (0 dB)
- Purpose: Deliver your message
- Tools: ElevenLabs, AI voices

**Layer 2: Sound Effects** (Impact)
- Volume: 60-80% (-3 to -6 dB)
- Purpose: Emphasize key moments
- Categories: Transitions, Emphasis, UI/Tech, Emotional, Nature

**Layer 3: Ambient Noise** (Atmosphere)
- Volume: 20-40% (-12 to -18 dB)
- Purpose: Create immersive environment
- Scenarios: Office, Coffee shop, Lab, Nature, Urban

## 🚀 Viral Video Formula

### What Makes Videos Go Viral

**The Formula**:
```
Viral Video = Hook × Value × Emotion × Shareability × Timing
```

**The 7-Second Rule**:
```
0-1s:  Viewer decides to keep watching or scroll
1-3s:  Brain processes if content is relevant
3-5s:  Emotional response triggered
5-7s:  Decision to engage (like, comment, share)

Result: 7 seconds = 80% of your success
```

1. **Shock Value**: "I made $50,000 in 24 hours..."

## 📊 Platform-Specific Strategies

### YouTube Shorts (1M+ Views)

**Optimal Structure**:
```
0-3s:   Hook (visual + audio shock)
3-10s:  Promise (what they'll learn)
10-45s: Deliver (3-5 key points)
45-55s: Climax (best tip/reveal)
55-60s: CTA (subscribe, comment)
```

**Posting Strategy**:
- Frequency: 3-5 Shorts per day
- Best times: 12-3 PM, 6-9 PM
- Hashtags: #Shorts + 2-3 niche tags
