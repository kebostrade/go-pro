## 💰 Monetization Strategies

### Revenue Streams You'll Learn

1. **YouTube AdSense**
   - Requirements: 1,000 subscribers, 4,000 watch hours
   - Potential: $2-$10 per 1,000 views
   - Strategy: Long-form content (8-15 minutes)

2. **TikTok Creator Fund**
   - Requirements: 10,000 followers, 100,000 views/30 days
   - Potential: $0.02-$0.04 per 1,000 views
   - Strategy: Viral shorts, trending sounds

3. **Sponsorships & Brand Deals**
   - Requirements: 10,000+ engaged followers
   - Potential: $100-$10,000+ per video
   - Strategy: Niche authority, engagement

4. **Affiliate Marketing**
   - Requirements: Any audience size
   - Potential: 5-30% commission per sale
   - Strategy: Product reviews, tutorials

5. **Digital Products**
   - Requirements: Expertise in niche
   - Potential: $10-$1,000+ per product
   - Strategy: Templates, presets, courses

6. **Course Creation**
   - Requirements: Proven results
   - Potential: $50-$500+ per student
   - Strategy: Teach your AI workflow
